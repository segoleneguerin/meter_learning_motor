There will be 2 types of trials: listening and clapping.


During LISTENING trials, please instruct the participant to:

	1. Relax, sit back and enjoy the sound.

	2. Prevent any movement synchronised with the rhythm
	   (e.g. air-tapping their toes).

	3. Keep their eyes fixated on a point when the sound is on.


During CLAPPING trials, please instruct the participant to:

	1. Clap the steady pulse of rhythm, similar 
	   to tapping their foot or bobbing their head to music.

	2. They can move other part of their body if this is of any help.
